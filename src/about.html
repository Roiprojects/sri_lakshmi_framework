About Sri Lakshmi Studio

Located in the heart of Bengaluru, Sri Lakshmi Photo Frame Works & Photo Studio is your trusted destination for premium imaging and framing solutions. We specialize in high-quality photo frames, professional photography services, and custom corporate gifting products.

Our craftsmanship brings together years of expertise with modern techniques, ensuring every frame, photo, and gift is delivered with precision and care. Whether it's a passport photo for your travels or a custom collage for your home, we are here to serve you.

Our Story

Every great creation starts with a vision. Sri Lakshmi Photo Frame Works began with a simple mission: to provide the people of Bengaluru with a place where their memories could be treated with the respect and artistry they deserve.

What started as a small framing workshop has grown into a full-service photo studio and corporate gifting hub. Our journey has been defined by a relentless commitment to quality and a passion for detail. Focus on quality, trust, and customer satisfaction is at the core of everything we do.
